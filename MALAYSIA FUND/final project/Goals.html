   From the research and surveys, we have gathered enough information and data to create statistics about the age group of people who know and give a helping hand to the MalaysiaFund or as we know it as MF.

In fact, there are come by a few reasons behind the back which are out of capacity and lack of funds to donate since they have just graduated from secondary school. On the other hand, they are just starting to prepare for jobs and further studies, even though there is also a portion of people who decided to proceed with studies in another country. Therefore, they are currently in debt due to taking out a loan from the government if they apply for the PTPTN. However, there are a minority of young adults who know about MF will donate a small amount of money to them or even become a volunteer to widely disseminate ideas to decrease those problems that occur consistently. From that 9 per cent of people, we know that they are affected by the inspiring talks from the seminar, which was organized by primary and secondary schools. As a result, some of the students will feel sympathy and much more connected to ecology.
